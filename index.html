Born in Venezuela, I embarked on a journey that took me overseas, where I have called various places home for over two decades. My artistic soul finds inspiration in the captivating blend of nature and architecture that surrounds me. During my time in Brazil, the vibrant contrasts, rich culture, and the beauty of dark skin tones akin to my own became powerful muses for my art. Immersed in the lively atmosphere of Bahia and Rio, I created art pieces that echoed the spirit of these places. I found fascination in ethnic details, from the vivid hues of clothing to intricate beads and blooming flowers, which found their way into my creations. Currently residing in the Netherlands, I have fallen deeply in love with Dutch architecture. The charming Dutch houses and iconic windmills have become integral elements in my artistic exploration. To further express my vision, I delved into the world of Acrylic paint, undertaking courses that allowed me to master the techniques of this challenging, fast-drying medium. Through dedication and passion, I've achieved a final product that possesses the aesthetic allure of oil painting. As I continue to reside in the Netherlands, my art reflects the dynamic fusion of my Venezuelan roots, Brazilian experiences, and newfound Dutch inspirations. Each stroke on canvas tells a unique story, a narrative that encapsulates the diverse influences that have shaped my artistic journey.
